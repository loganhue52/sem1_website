 The Bander Believers Society is a group of high minded indivuals who worship the one and only Lord Bander. Lord Bander, known as Nic Bander, is a highly praised and beloved teacher at the Southern Indiana Career and Technical Center.


Our School-Appropriate Rituals
  • The Destruction Ritual is an Office Space type ritual where we get together and destroy old, slow, and ancient piece of technology. Such technology consists of old high school Dell Optiplex computers, any beige-colored printer, or even broken Kurigs. Baseball bats will be provided.
  • The Rubby Ducky Prayer Ritual is one of our most sacred rituals. A large (10ft x 10ft), yellow rubber duck is inflated and we gather in a perfectly even circle around the duck. We stand, bow our heads, and recite a latin phrase which will tell the rubby ducky gods to give Lord Bander good luck and rich fortune. It is definitely not an ancient latin prayer to summon a god. No need to worry, pieces of old Yellow Pages books will be provided with the prayer written on them over the old Yellow Pages text.
  • The Bandermon Ritual is a fun card game similar to Pokemon where each Pokemon's face has been replaced the same picture of Lord Bander on each card. Players will hold duels against each other in a tournament style. Instead of gambling with real money, because everybody is broke is this day and age, we bet on the winner with Kurig Cups, known as K-Cups. Each K-Cup is equal to one dollar.
